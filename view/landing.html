JCC History

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) located in Jakarta, Indonesia is a renowned convention center. First Opened in 1974, JCC hold many important national and international event. Throughout the years, JCC has hosted numerous high-profile events, including the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference. It remains a highly sought-after venue throughout the year.
