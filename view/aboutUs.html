The Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a well-known convention center situated in Jakarta, Indonesia. Established in 1974, JCC has become a prominent venue for hosting various significant national and international events.

Over the years, it has successfully organized numerous prestigious gatherings, such as the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference.
